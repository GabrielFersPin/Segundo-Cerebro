**SageMaker JumpStart**: Helps to find projects that are already built and are quick builds with datasets, models, algorithm types, and solutions based on industry best practices. It's a model hub and it helps quickly deploy foundation models, and also helps you get quickly into production to operate at scale. It provides fine-tunning and deploying models, and also helps you get quickly into production to operate at scale. JumpStart also provides a lot of resources in terms of blogs, videos, and example notebooks. JumpStart models require GPUs to fine-tune and deploy. 

**AWS Nitro System**: Has specialized hardware and associated firmware that are designed to enforce security restrictions. These restrictions are to ensure that no one can access your workloads or data running on Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud, EC2 instances.

**Amazon Titan**: Is a FM provided by Amazon acts as a general-purpose foundation model and is a great option if you require tax generation capabilities.

**Amazon Bedrock**: offers features that can help you do just that throught the use of playgrounds and model evaluations.

	Amazon Bedrock Playgrounds: Let you experiment by runnig model inference against different base foundation models that are supported within the service to help you align your use cases with the highest accuracy.

	Amazon Bedrock Party Rock: You can use it to build generative AI applications to learn fundamental techniques, such as understanding how a foundational model responds to different prompts. Some examples of applications that you can build in PartyRock are creating playlists, trivia, game, recipes, and more. .
